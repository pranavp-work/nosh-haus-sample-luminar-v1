About Us

At Nosh Haus Cafe, we pride ourselves on providing a sophisticated yet serene atmosphere that makes every visit memorable. Our menu boasts a range of specialty coffees, including the much-loved Honey Orgeat Latte and the striking Charcoal Latte. Alongside our coffee, we offer a variety of dishes from fresh pizzas and juicy burgers to healthy salads and indulgent desserts. Known for our posh and calming environment, Nosh Haus is not just a cafe—it's a retreat. Our late hours make us an ideal choice for those seeking a late-night spot, while our music and ambiance create the perfect backdrop for relaxation and enjoyment.
